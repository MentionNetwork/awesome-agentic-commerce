# Amazon Seller Tools — Further Reading

The best writing on Amazon's shift into agentic commerce. Newest-first.

- [Amazon Ditches Rufus Chatbot, Launches Alexa Shopping Agent in AI Strategy Pivot](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/13/amazon-ditches-rufus-ai-chatbot-in-favor-of-alexa-shopping-agent.html) - CNBC, 2026. Amazon retired Rufus in favor of "Alexa for Shopping," a personalized agentic assistant marking its shift from chatbot Q&A to full shopping agents.
- [Amazon Opens Its Ad Stack to AI Agents With MCP Rollout](https://www.adweek.com/media/amazon-agentic-ads-model-context-protocol/) - Adweek, 2026. Amazon's Ads MCP Server open beta lets advertisers run campaign creation, reporting and optimization through AI agents via a single Model Context Protocol integration.
- [Amazon Edges Deeper Into Agentic Commerce With Rufus 'Auto Buy' Function](https://www.emarketer.com/content/amazon-edges-deeper-agentic-commerce-rufus-auto-buy) - eMarketer, 2025. Rufus's Auto Buy and Buy for Me features show Amazon moving from AI product discovery to autonomous purchasing on and off its own marketplace.
