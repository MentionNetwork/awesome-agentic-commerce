# Dropshipping & Sourcing — Further Reading

The best writing on dropshipping automation and AI. Newest-first.

- [AI Dropshipping: What It Is and Top Tools to Use](https://www.shopify.com/blog/ai-dropshipping) - Shopify, 2026. Breaks down where AI actually moves the needle in dropshipping — product research, listing generation and support automation — noting 71% of AI-attributed Shopify orders came from long-tail niches in 2025.
- [10 Best Automated Dropshipping Tools to Know in 2026](https://www.shopify.com/blog/automated-dropshipping) - Shopify, 2026. Practical comparison of automation tools covering order routing, inventory sync and pricing rules for hands-off store operations.
- [Doba Launches Unified AI Platform to Consolidate Every Dropshipping Automation Tool in One Place](https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/technology/articles/doba-launches-unified-ai-platform-050100896.html) - Yahoo Finance, 2025. Signals the shift to agentic commerce: sellers issue a single natural-language instruction and the platform executes the full workflow.
