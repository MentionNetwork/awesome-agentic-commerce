# Marketing, SEO & GEO — Further Reading

The best writing on ecommerce marketing, SEO, and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — getting your brand and products cited by AI answer engines. Newest-first.

- [The GEO Playbook: How (& Why) to Optimize for AI Discovery](https://www.shopify.com/enterprise/blog/generative-engine-optimization) - Shopify, 2026. Reports AI-referred orders grew nearly 13x year over year and lays out steps to get products surfaced by ChatGPT and Google AI Mode.
- [Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): The Comprehensive Guide](https://cxl.com/blog/answer-engine-optimization-aeo-the-comprehensive-guide/) - CXL, 2026. Reframes optimization from rankings to citations, with a multi-source strategy across Reddit, YouTube and review sites.
- [The Complete Guide to Ecommerce AI SEO](https://www.triplewhale.com/blog/ai-ecommerce-seo) - Triple Whale, 2026. Practical ecommerce guide to infrastructure, content and off-site authority for AI-driven shopping search.
- [How Ecommerce Brands Actually Get Discovered in AI Search](https://searchengineland.com/guide/ecommerce-ai) - Search Engine Land, 2026. Argues AI discovery depends on consensus signals across Reddit, YouTube, reviews and retailer pages, not a brand's own site alone.
- [How AI-Driven Shopping Discovery Changes Product Page Optimization](https://searchengineland.com/ai-driven-shopping-discovery-product-page-optimization-468765) - Search Engine Land, 2026. Product pages must supply constraint-matching attributes rather than keyword copy to win agent recommendations.
- [Your Product Data Is Holding Back Your Agentic Commerce Strategy](https://commercetools.com/blog/ai-ready-product-data-for-agentic-commerce-success) - commercetools, 2026. Lays out a four-layer product-data model so AI agents can reliably discover and recommend products.
- [Top Brand Visibility Factors in ChatGPT, AI Mode, and AI Overviews](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-brand-visibility-correlations/) - Ahrefs, 2025. Study of 75,000 brands finding YouTube and branded web mentions are the strongest predictors of appearing in AI answers.
- [Optimizing Product Feeds for AI Overviews, LLMs, and Google Merchant Center](https://www.marceldigital.com/blog/optimizing-product-feeds-for-ai-overviews-llms-and-google-merchant-center) - Marcel Digital, 2025. Shows product feeds now extend into AI-driven discovery and need richer structured data to stay visible.
- [GEO: Generative Engine Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735) - arXiv (Princeton, Georgia Tech, Allen AI, IIT Delhi), 2024. Foundational KDD 2024 paper coining "GEO"; adding statistics, citations and quotations can lift visibility in generative answers by up to 40%.
- [How to Use Ecommerce LLMs.txt to Boost AI Discoverability](https://www.bigcommerce.com/blog/ecommerce-llms-txt/) - BigCommerce. Explains the emerging llms.txt convention for feeding clean, structured product data to LLMs.
