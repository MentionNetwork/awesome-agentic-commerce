# Top Shopify Apps — Further Reading

The best writing on the Shopify app ecosystem and how AI is reshaping it. Newest-first.

- [Agentic Commerce: Benefits & How To Get Started](https://www.shopify.com/blog/agentic-commerce) - Shopify, 2026. Explains how AI agents now browse, compare and buy on shoppers' behalf, and how merchants should prepare catalogs and checkout.
- [The 15 Best Shopify Apps in 2026](https://zapier.com/blog/best-shopify-apps/) - Zapier, 2026. Hands-on tested roundup of the top merchant apps across reviews, upsells, support and marketing.
- [The Agentic Commerce Platform: Shopify Connects Any Merchant to Every AI Conversation](https://www.shopify.com/news/ai-commerce-at-scale) - Shopify, 2025. Announces Shopify's integrations that surface merchant catalogs and checkout inside OpenAI, Microsoft Copilot and other AI assistants.
