# Understanding Agentic Commerce — Further Reading

The best writing on what agentic commerce is, the protocols behind it, and how the market is taking shape. Newest-first.

- [Under the Hood: Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP)](https://developers.googleblog.com/under-the-hood-universal-commerce-protocol-ucp/) - Google Developers, 2026. Technical breakdown of UCP's primitives — Checkout, Identity Linking, Order and Payment Token Exchange.
- [Building the Universal Commerce Protocol](https://shopify.engineering/UCP) - Shopify Engineering, 2026. Shopify's account of co-developing UCP with Google and wiring it into agentic checkout.
- [New Tech and Tools for Retailers to Succeed in an Agentic Shopping Era](https://blog.google/products/ads-commerce/agentic-commerce-ai-tools-protocol-retailers-platforms/) - Google, 2026. Introduces the Shopify-Google Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) plus Business Agent and Direct Offers for agentic shopping.
- [Agentic Commerce: AI Shopping Agents Guide](https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/industries/consumer/articles/agentic-commerce-ai-shopping-agents-guide.html) - Deloitte, 2026. Analyst overview of how AI agents integrate with retailer systems and reshape purchasing behavior.
- [What Is Agentic Commerce? A Complete Guide for 2026](https://gr4vy.com/posts/what-is-agentic-commerce-a-complete-guide-for-2026/) - GR4VY, 2026. End-to-end primer on agent-driven purchasing and the infrastructure merchants need.
- [Agentic Commerce Trends and Statistics for 2026](https://www.metarouter.io/post/agentic-commerce-trends-statistics) - MetaRouter, 2026. Data-backed snapshot of adoption, spend and market size.
- [AI Trends Shaping Agentic Commerce](https://commercetools.com/blog/ai-trends-shaping-agentic-commerce) - commercetools, 2026. How autonomous agents are redefining digital retail.
- [Developing an Open Standard for Agentic Commerce](https://stripe.com/blog/developing-an-open-standard-for-agentic-commerce) - Stripe, 2025. Stripe and OpenAI's announcement of the Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) powering Instant Checkout in ChatGPT.
- [Buy It in ChatGPT: Instant Checkout and the Agentic Commerce Protocol](https://openai.com/index/buy-it-in-chatgpt/) - OpenAI, 2025. Launch post for in-chat purchasing, the consumer debut of agentic checkout in a mainstream AI assistant.
- [Announcing the Agent Payments Protocol (AP2)](https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/announcing-agents-to-payments-ap2-protocol) - Google Cloud, 2025. Open, payment-agnostic protocol using signed "Mandates" to prove a human authorized an agent's purchase.
