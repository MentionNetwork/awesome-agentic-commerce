# WooCommerce & WordPress — Top Articles & Guides

The best articles, tutorials and guides for building and running a WooCommerce store on WordPress — for developers and store owners alike.

## For Store Owners

- [WooCommerce Made Simple: Proven Steps to Launch Your Store](https://www.wpbeginner.com/wp-tutorials/woocommerce-tutorial-ultimate-guide/) - WPBeginner, 2025. Comprehensive beginner walkthrough from hosting and install through products, payments, tax and shipping.
- [Start with WooCommerce in 5 Steps](https://woocommerce.com/document/start-with-woocommerce-in-5-steps/) - WooCommerce (Automattic). Official quickstart covering hosting, install, plugin activation, Storefront theme and extensions.
- [Ecommerce SEO in 2026: What to Know When You're Starting Out](https://woocommerce.com/posts/seo-basics-ecommerce-beginners-guide/) - WooCommerce, 2026. Official beginner SEO guide on product descriptions, keyword intent, site architecture and indexing.
- [Product Page SEO: 5 Things to Improve](https://yoast.com/product-page-seo/) - Yoast, 2025. Focused checklist for product-page titles, descriptions, metadata, structured data and page speed.

## For Developers

- [How to Build Your First Extension](https://developer.woocommerce.com/docs/extensions/getting-started-extensions/building-your-first-extension/) - WooCommerce Developer Docs. Official guide to scaffolding an extension with create-woo-extension and a wp-env local dev environment.
- [Headless WooCommerce: From Setup to Deployment](https://kinsta.com/blog/headless-woocommerce/) - Kinsta, 2026. End-to-end guide to decoupling WooCommerce with a React frontend over the REST API.
- [18 Powerful Ways to Speed Up Your WooCommerce Store](https://kinsta.com/blog/speed-up-woocommerce/) - Kinsta, 2026. Actionable performance guide spanning hosting, caching, image optimization and database cleanup.

## Platform Choice

- [WooCommerce vs Shopify: Which Is Better for Your Online Store?](https://kinsta.com/blog/woocommerce-vs-shopify/) - Kinsta, 2026. Strategy comparison across setup, customization, pricing and security with market-share data.
