# Top WooCommerce Plugins — Further Reading

The best writing on WooCommerce and its agentic commerce roadmap. Newest-first.

- [AI Is Changing How Shoppers Find Your Products](https://woocommerce.com/posts/ai-product-discovery/) - WooCommerce, 2026. Argues AI agents now search, compare and buy on shoppers' behalf, so merchants must keep product data clean and structured to stay discoverable via MCP, ACP and UCP.
- [AI & Agentic Commerce in WooCommerce Roadmap](https://developer.woocommerce.com/2025/10/03/ai-agentic-commerce-in-woocommerce/) - WooCommerce Developer Blog, 2025. Lays out Woo's official roadmap for agentic commerce: a core MCP server for catalog search and cart creation, agent-readable product data, and ACP-based agent checkout.
